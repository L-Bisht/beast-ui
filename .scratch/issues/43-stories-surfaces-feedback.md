## What to build

Write Storybook stories for surface and feedback components: Surface, Card, Collapsible, Alert, Spinner, Progress, Skeleton.

## Acceptance criteria

- [x] Surface story shows all elevation levels, radius, border
- [x] Card story shows elevated/outlined, clickable, with header/footer/media
- [x] Collapsible story shows expand/collapse animation, controlled mode, disabled
- [x] Alert story shows all severity × variant combinations, closable, with title
- [x] Spinner story shows all sizes and colors
- [x] Progress story shows determinate, indeterminate, with showValue
- [x] Skeleton story shows text/circular/rectangular variants, animation modes, multi-line
- [x] Stories organized under "Surfaces" and "Feedback" categories
